information from R & D

Recent past burden of lifestyle and metabolic disorders have increased universally, especially in country like India. According to Indian Council of Medical Research, the estimated proportion of all deaths due to Non-Communicable Diseases (NCDs) has increased from 37.09% in 1990 to 61.8% in 2016. Therefore, health & wellness product industry became a most emerging industry of India from the last decade. Health & medical science industry is becoming more specialized day by day but on the other hand, health & wellness product market in India is very general and non-specialized. We need to develop more specialized and customized products according to demand drives of customers. Our research and development team analyzed the data of several reputed Indian & International health science organizations for products development. Our research and development team come with specialized products that are customized according to burdens of lifestyle disorder.

India might be the world’s third country on socio-economic development parameters in terms of our lifestyle and in health parameters we have the same morbidity profile of a first world nation. Lifestyle diseases like cardiovascular, diabetes, hypertension, asthma, obesity, arteriosclerosis, stroke, respiratory as well as cancers are on the rise. The World Health Organization (WHO) identifies alcohol, tobacco, poor diet intake, pesticides, and air pollutants, foods that are high in salt, sugar, and fat and low in nutrition and lack of physical activity as major risk factors for NCDs (non-communicable diseases).

Today human beings are suffering from hidden hunger that is deficiency of essential micronutrients. We can fulfill our hunger by simply intake of food but hidden hunger doesn’t feel like routine food hunger. It is only diagnosed on an onset of any lifestyle disorder. Therefore, our aims are attainment of the highest possible level of good health and well-being for all at all ages, through a preventive and promote health and wellness products.

Recently our R & D team developed a unique concept of products i.e Herbo-foodceutical. It is a unique formulation consisting of a mixture of powerful herbal extracts with essential nutrients to fight against specific lifestyle disorders like Brain fuel for brain wellness, Thyro fuel for thyroid function, heart fuel for cardiac wellness, Beauty Fuel for skin wellness, Liver fuel for liver wellness etc. The range is formulated according to customer’s specialized requirements of today’s health world.

In India, maximum health & wellness supplements are very general and non-specialized but our company developed a premium range of products that is Herbo-foodceutical which enables us to manage our specialized health issues with specialized products. When products are of general category in direct selling industry; as a distributor it is very difficult to understand and distribute and as a consumer it is very difficult to decide which one is better to manage their specific requirements. Therefore, our company has always been known for its unique and quality researched products of modern health & wellness world.
